Service Process

Product Distribution

We act as intermediaries between manufacturers and retailers, supplying electrical products to retailers, contractors, electricians, and other businesses in the electrical industry. We maintain a vast inventory of electrical supplies and equipment from various brands and manufacturers.

Delivery & Logistics

We provide delivery services to transport electrical products to customer locations. We have logistics systems in place to efficiently handle orders, arrange shipping or delivery, and track the status of shipments.



Credit and Financing

We offer credit terms or financing options to eligible customers, providing flexibility in payment arrangements. This can be particularly beneficial for contractors or businesses with ongoing projects that require a consistent supply of electrical products.


Product Sourcing


Raj Line have established relationships with multiple manufacturers and suppliers, allowing them to source and procure electrical products based on customer requirements. We help customers find specific products or recommend alternatives if needed.

Bulk Purchasing


Raj Line as a wholesaler offers bulk purchasing options, allowing customers to buy products in larger quantities at discounted prices. This is particularly beneficial for contractors and businesses that require a high volume of electrical supplies for their projects.

Warehousing & Inventory Management

We maintain warehouses and storage facilities to store an extensive inventory of electrical products. We are responsible for managing inventory levels, tracking stock, and ensuring the availability of products to meet customer demands.
