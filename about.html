About Us

Welcome to RAJ LINE - Electrical goods and Home appliances brand! We are a company dedicated to providing you with high-quality products that will help you make your life more comfortable, efficient, and enjoyable.

At Raj Line, we understand that electrical goods and home appliances are an essential part of modern life. They make our lives easier, more productive, and more enjoyable. That's why we are committed to providing you with products that are not only of high quality but also stylish and functional.

We offer a wide range of products, from small kitchen appliances to large home entertainment systems. Our product range includes energy-efficient appliances, smart home technology, and the latest electrical gadgets that are designed to make your life easier and more convenient.

Raj Line is proud to be a brand that you can trust. We use the latest technology and the highest-quality materials to manufacture our products, ensuring that they are durable, reliable, and long-lasting. We are constantly improving our products and services to ensure that we meet your changing needs and preferences.

We believe that customer satisfaction is the cornerstone of our success. That's why we provide exceptional customer service, from the moment you contact us to the time the product arrives at your doorstep. Our team is always ready to answer your questions, provide advice, and help you make the right purchase decision.

Thank you for choosing Raj Line. We look forward to serving you and helping you make your life more comfortable, efficient, and enjoyable!

Our Story

Raj Line brand started with a simple vision - to provide high-quality products that are both stylish and functional, making everyday life easier and more enjoyable.


It all began when our founder, the Late. Shri Prakashchand Idandas Jethani a seasoned entrepreneur with a passion for technology, noticed a gap in the market for electrical goods and home appliances that were both affordable and high-quality. With this vision in mind, our brand was born in 1994.


From the start, Raj Line has been committed to using the latest technology and the highest-quality materials to manufacture our products. We understand that electrical goods and home appliances are an essential part of modern life, and we believe that they should be both efficient and aesthetically pleasing.


Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted name in the industry, known for our innovative products and exceptional customer service. We have expanded our product range to include energy-efficient appliances, smart home technology, and the latest electrical gadgets, always with the aim of making your life easier and more convenient.


At the heart of our brand is a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about what they do. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that we meet your changing needs and preferences, providing exceptional customer service every step of the way.


Today, we are proud to have a loyal customer base that spans all over the Maharashtra. We are constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible, always striving to provide you with the very best in electrical goods and home appliances.


As we look to the future, we remain committed to our founding vision - to make your life more comfortable, efficient, and enjoyable with our innovative products and exceptional customer service. Thank you for choosing Raj Line - Electrical goods and home appliances.
